Getting to Know Me a Little Bit Better!

Hello! Currently a Digital Media Undergraduate Student at UMD College Park, I strive to continuously develop my artistic vision, whether it be on paper or laptop. The goal: to make it into the art industry.

Once a person in the medical field, I decided to follow my passion for art and went towards my Bachelor's in Digital Media. Life is too short and seeing an opportunity to educate myself as an artist, I took it. While pursuing a Bachelor's, I spend my time outside of academia taking inspiration from the world around me. Examples include visiting art museums, watching tv shows, and building DIY miniature housekits. Hayao Miyazaki (renowned co-founder/director of Studio Ghibli Inc.) would be listed as my top artistic inspiration, being a big fan of the Studio Ghibli films.
